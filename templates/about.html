About LeakCalcs.io

Built by Engineers, for Engineers.

LeakCalcs.io was founded to solve a specific problem in the HVAC-R manufacturing industry: The complexity of converting refrigerant leak rates to helium test parameters.

Our Background

I am a Mechanical Engineer with over 5 years of hands-on experience in the HVAC-R sector, specifically focusing on quality control, leak detection systems, and production line optimization.

Throughout my career, I noticed that many quality managers and R&D engineers rely on scattered Excel sheets or outdated manuals to perform critical ASTM conversions. This often leads to errors in Helium Leak Detector settings, resulting in either false passes or unnecessary rejections.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a standard, reliable, and accessible tool for the industry. LeakCalcs.io eliminates the guesswork by using precise thermodynamic equations (EOS) to calculate viscosity and pressure differentials instantly.
